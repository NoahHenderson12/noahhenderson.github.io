SIGMA PHI EPSILON



What is Sigma Phi Epsilon?
A fraternity

SigEp is a Greek organization, or a fraternity. It is a national organization, meaning that there is a "headquarters" that helps establish new chapters across the U.S and helps support the active chapers.

What are their values?
Virtue, Dilligence, and Brotherly Love

SigEp prides itself on creating "balanced men". SigEp does this by focusing on 3 core principles: Virtue, Dilligence, and Brotherly Love. These three principles are what SigEp lives by, and what each member strives to understand.

My experience
Why I'm here

I joined SigEp with the hope that I could be a part of a community on campus, as I didn't have one when I first arrived. Not only did I get a community, I got a group of guys that shared the same values as me, were as dedicated to acedemics as me, and who were open and willing to grow into better men.
